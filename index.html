Story-driven exploration in a fully destructible world

Caves is a story-driven mining game in a fully destructible world. Cut through solid rock to uncover lost technology and ancient creatures. Build a base, make strange friends and find a way to reclaim your lost home.

Key Features

  • Explore a fully destructible polygon world
  • Befriend strange creatures and learn their secrets
  • Develop new technologies to build your base
  • Follow the main quest to save your people, or freely explore and make your own story

Platform

PC and Mac.
